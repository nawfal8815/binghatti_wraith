WHY Binghatti Wraith?

Binghatti Wraith redefines contemporary urban living through its striking crystalline glass façades and panoramic vistas of Dubai’s most iconic landmarks, including Burj Khalifa, Dubai Frame, and the city’s skyline. Strategically positioned in the heart of Al Jaddaf, it is just 6 minutes away from Downtown Dubai, offering seamless connectivity to DIFC, Dubai Healthcare City, and Dubai International Airport, while placing residents moments away from the cultural richness of the Mohammed Bin Rashid Library and Dubai Design District (D3).

Frequently Asked Questions

What property types are available at Binghatti Wraith?

Binghatti Wraith offers a diverse range of property types, including Studio, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom apartments, along with ground-floor retail spaces..

How many units and floors does Binghatti Wraith have?

Binghatti Wraith comprises the ground floor, 4 Podium Levels, 14 Residential Floors, and a Roof.

Where is Binghatti Wraith located?

Binghatti Wraith is strategically situated in Al-Jaddaf, offering close proximity to Downtown Dubai, Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai Healthcare City, and Dubai International Airport. Nearby key destinations include:

  • Downtown Dubai - 6 minutes
  • Dubai International Airport - 8 minutes
  • Zabeel Palace - 8 minutes
  • DIFC - 10 minutes

How accessible is Binghatti Wraith to major roads and transport?

Binghatti Wraith enjoys excellent connectivity to Dubai's major routes, including:

  • E11, Sheikh Zayed Road
  • E44, Al Khail Road
  • Oud Metha (Dubai – Ain)

Residents also benefit from convenient access to the Metro, Uber, and taxi services.

What schools and educational facilities are near Binghatti Wraith?

Binghatti Wraith is located close to a selection of esteemed international schools and daycare facilities, including:

  • Swiss International Scientific School in Dubai
  • Dubai Gem Private School
  • Grammar School
  • Almawakeb School – Garhoud

What amenities does Binghatti Wraith offer?

Binghatti Wraith offers an exceptional array of lifestyle and wellness amenities, including:

  • Infinity Pool, Jacuzzi & Private Cabanas
  • Spa & Wellness Area
  • Gym, Fitness Centre & Yoga Deck
  • Padel Court & Half Basketball Court
  • Palm Promenade & Landscaped Gardens
  • Family BBQ Area & Social Gathering Spaces
  • Children's Play Areas
  • EV Charging Stations

What cultural attractions are near Binghatti Wraith?

Binghatti Wraith is positioned close to Dubai's leading cultural landmarks, including the Mohammed Bin Rashid Library and the Dubai Design District (D3), placing residents at the centre of the city's creative and intellectual hubs.
